F.A.Q.

What is this?

Well, I noticed that on /r/pcmasterrace there were lots of giveaways of Steam keys. However, they worked solely on a basis of first-come-first-serve, and there was always a chance of the key being sniped by a bot. So, I made this site.

How does it work?

Here's how a giveaway works here:

  1. The person who wants to give away a key creates a new giveaway.
  2. The giver gets a public URL to post on reddit, or wherever they want to do the giveaway.
  3. When someone visits the URL, they are met with a CAPTCHA. When they fill out the CAPTCHA correctly, they have a 1/100 chance (or any chance set by the giveaway creator) of getting the key.
  4. People continue visiting the URL and filling out the CAPTCHA in hopes of getting the key. When someone finally does, they are prompted for a thank you message, and can optionally supply their reddit username. This information is then displayed on the now-closed giveaway page
  5. The giver feels warm and fuzzy, and some lucky person enjoys a new game
